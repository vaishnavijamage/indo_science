ABOUT INDO SCIENCE

Indo Science Education Trust, a NON PROFIT, strives to bridge the gap between theoretical and practical STEM knowledge. Our mission is to empower the next generation of professionals by providing hands-on experiences and fostering scientific curiosity from an early age.

Indo Science Today

Indo Science inspires young minds by turning curiosity into achievement. Through research, education, and innovation, we continue to impact thousands of students across the country, shaping the future of scientific discovery.

Looking Forward

Our vision is to expand Indo Science’s reach by introducing advanced programs, new learning technologies, and global collaborations. We aim to build future leaders who will drive innovation and exploration.

A History of Innovation

Indo Science Education Trust is a non‑governmental trust (NGO) registered in Maharashtra, established on 19 April 2012.The organization’s mission: to promote science & technology education, vocational training, literacy — especially through STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, Mathematics) initiatives and outreach.
