Spark

Spark is designed to ignite curiosity among students through engaging science experiments, interactive learning, and innovative approaches.

Knowledge Visit

Knowledge Visits allow students to explore real-world applications of science and technology by visiting research centers, industries, and science exhibitions.

Science & Tech Activity

These activities aim to enhance creativity and technical skills in students by involving them in competitions, workshops, and innovation challenges.At Indo Science Education Trust, we are committed to igniting a passion for science and technology among students through engaging and interactive activities.

Rocketry

Our Rocketry Program is designed to inspire young minds with a passion for aerospace and exploration. Through hands-on experiences and practical learning, students delve into the fascinating world of rockets and space travel.

STEAM For School

What is STEAM Education? STEAM is an acronym that stands for Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics. This innovative approach to education goes beyond traditional STEM disciplines by incorporating the arts.
